Registration Procedure

  1. Login into https://atalacademy.aicte-india.org/login

  2. Navigate to the FDP tab.

  3. Under Filter FDPs:

    • Select Type as "ATAL".
    • Choose Month as "December".
    • Set Thrust Area to "ALL". Choose Mode as Online.

  4. Press Ctrl + F to open the search function, enter the application number 1730701212, and press Enter to find the desired FDP.

  5. Click the "+" button to apply for the selected FDP. If you are not a registered user, please register as a participant first and then follow the steps above.

Who Can Participate?

  • No registration fee for participation.

  • Selection will be done on a first-come, first-serve basis and confirmed candidates will be notified immediately.

  • A test shall be conducted (online) by the coordinator at the end of the program.

  • The certificates shall be issued to those participants who are registered on www.aicte-india.org/atal and attend the program with a minimum 80% attendance and score minimum 70% marks in the test.
